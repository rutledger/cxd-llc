Home Safety SMS Alerts

Consent, privacy, and terms for home safety SMS alerts.

Business Entity

Customer Experience Delivered LLC is a private, for-profit Colorado limited liability company. Customer Experience Delivered LLC is the legal entity associated with this toll-free number.

Customer Experience Delivered LLC provides business consulting services focused on customer experience and contact center operations. This toll-free number is used only for low-volume, non-marketing home safety SMS alerts administered by an authorized representative of Customer Experience Delivered LLC.

Public business registration: Colorado Secretary of State business registration for Customer Experience Delivered LLC

Purpose of Messages

Customer Experience Delivered LLC uses this toll-free number only to send private home automation safety and status alerts from a Hubitat home automation system to household and family members who have directly agreed to receive these messages.

These messages are not marketing, promotional, or commercial. They are used only for private household safety and status notifications.

Message Types

Messages may include:

  • Water leak alerts
  • Freezer or refrigerator temperature alerts
  • Door, lock, or alarm status alerts
  • Device failure or safety-related home automation alerts
  • Other urgent home automation alerts related to household safety or property protection

Message Frequency

Message frequency is low and event-based. Messages are sent only when a monitored home automation event triggers an alert. Expected message volume is approximately 100 messages per month or fewer.

Opt-In and Consent

Recipients are added only after direct consent.

Opt-In Process

Each recipient provides their phone number directly to an authorized representative of Customer Experience Delivered LLC and verbally agrees to receive Customer Experience Delivered LLC Hubitat home safety SMS alerts before their phone number is added to the Hubitat/Twilio notification list.

Phone numbers are manually added only after consent is given. No phone numbers are purchased, rented, scraped, imported from third-party lists, or obtained from any third-party lead source.

Verbal Consent Script

Before adding a recipient, an authorized representative of Customer Experience Delivered LLC asks:

"Do you agree to receive SMS alerts from Customer Experience Delivered LLC's Hubitat home automation system? These messages may include urgent home safety alerts such as water leaks, alarm events, door or lock status, freezer or refrigerator temperature warnings, or device failure alerts. Message frequency will be low and event-based. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP."

A recipient is added only if they agree.

Example Consent Flow

Authorized representative of Customer Experience Delivered LLC:

"Do you want to receive SMS alerts from Customer Experience Delivered LLC's Hubitat home automation system for urgent home safety events?"

Recipient:

"Yes."

Authorized representative of Customer Experience Delivered LLC:

"These alerts may include water leaks, alarm events, door or lock status, freezer or refrigerator temperature warnings, or device failure alerts. Message frequency will be low and event-based. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. Do you agree?"

Recipient:

"Yes."

The authorized representative then manually adds the recipient's phone number to the Hubitat/Twilio notification list.

Opt-In Confirmation Message

You are set up to receive Customer Experience Delivered LLC Hubitat home safety alerts. Alerts may include leaks, alarm events, locks, temperature, or device failures. Reply STOP to opt out.

Sample Alert Message

Customer Experience Delivered LLC Hubitat Alert: Upstairs toilet leak sensor is wet. Check immediately. Reply STOP to opt out.

Help Message

Customer Experience Delivered LLC Hubitat home safety alerts. For help, email rutledgedr@msn.com. Reply STOP to opt out.

Opt-Out

Recipients may opt out at any time by replying STOP. Once a recipient opts out, their phone number will be removed from the notification list and they will no longer receive these SMS alerts.

Privacy

Phone numbers are used only to send the home safety SMS alerts described on this page.

Phone numbers are not sold, rented, shared, or used for marketing. SMS opt-in consent and phone numbers are not shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.
